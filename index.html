What is Global Warming?

Climate around the world is highly affected by global warming, this affects the polar bears around the world. In Antarctica the climate is increasing causing the ice to melt. This is a problem because it affects the polar bears environment. It reduces access to food, increasing in drowning and lower cub surivial rates.
